怨 (Blame) Short Film

This short film explores the theme of severe bullying and its psychological toll, leading to the emergence of a deadly alter ego.

Story Summary

The main character transfers to a new school hoping for a fresh start after being bullied by his previous classmates. Unfortunately, a misunderstanding leads to him being bullied again at the new school. Overwhelmed by the trauma, he gradually develops a **second personality**.

This second personality, driven by revenge, begins **killing all the people who bullied him**. The narrative of the film is primarily delivered through a **conversation between the main character and a psychiatrist**.
