A Peace of Bun (Final Year Project)

This interactive installation was developed as a Final Year Project for the BScCM program. It focuses on the Cheung Chau Bun Festival to create a tangible, engaging experience promoting cultural heritage.

Project Focus

The project is an **Interactive Installation** designed to connect participants with the unique cultural elements of the Cheung Chau Jiao Festival, particularly the iconic **Bun Carnival**. The goal is often to transform cultural research into a playful, memorable, and accessible experience for modern audiences.
