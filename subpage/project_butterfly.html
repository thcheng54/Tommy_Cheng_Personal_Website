Butterfly Effect Story App

This project is an **interactive story app** where user choices significantly alter the narrative, based on the concept of the Butterfly Effect.

Project Background & Concept

The story background is a creative modification based on several classic horror movies and tales, including:

  • ‘Carved: The Slit-Mouthed Woman’ (2007)
  • ‘The Clown Statue’ (2012)
  • ‘It’ (2017)

The core mechanic involves **3 key questions** where the user can choose options to change the processes or results of the story. A total of **5 different endings** were designed to reflect the impact of these decisions.

The Plot and Crisis

The main antagonist, the **Clown**, acts as the 'monster in the house.' Depending on the user's choices, the victims can be **Sam and Maggie or Susanna**. The first crisis is set in **1950**, but the Clown's influence affects the victims' lives again **20 or 30 years later**, highlighting the long-term consequences of the initial decisions.
