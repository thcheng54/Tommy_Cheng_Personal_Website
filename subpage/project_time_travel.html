Time Travel

This short film uses a speculative concept—a timer on people's heads counting down their remaining lifespan—to convey a powerful social message.

Concept and Message

The film highlights that in a busy society, most people ignore the timer until the last few hours remain. At this critical moment, a small number of people finally stop and focus on cherishing the people around them. Through this video, I aim to promote a message of cherishing everyone around you.

Production Details and Special Effects

In the final two hours of his life shown in the film, the actor chooses to spend time with his closest friends and uses his camera to enjoy beautiful moments from the past.

  • Special Effects: The film uses special effects in the title display, where the actor can cross the font.
  • Transitions: Custom transitions were used to enhance the feeling of time travel and a future world.
  • Tracking: The short film features a FaceTime scene with the actor and his girlfriend, utilizing tracking techniques to contribute to the future world aesthetic.
